# Figure Sources

References for key cultural figures: founders, teachers, transformative figures.

## General Biographical

- Hornblower, Simon, and Antony Spawforth, eds. *The Oxford Classical Dictionary*. 4th ed. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2012.
  Classical world figures, dates, roles.

- *Encyclopaedia Britannica*. 15th ed. Chicago: Encyclopaedia Britannica, 2010.
  Cross-cultural biographical reference.

## Egyptian Figures

- Shaw (2000). Pharaonic figures, Imhotep (c. 2650 BCE), dating conventions.

## Persian Figures

- Brosius (2006). Cyrus the Great (c. 600--530 BCE), Zoroaster dating controversy.
- Boyce, Mary. *Zoroastrians: Their Religious Beliefs and Practices*. London: Routledge, 2001.
  Zoroaster traditional dating, role as founder.

## Indian Figures

- Basham (1967). Vedic sages, Buddha (c. 563--483 BCE), Ashoka.
- Thapar, Romila. *Ashoka and the Decline of the Mauryas*. 3rd ed. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2012.
  Ashoka dates, role in Buddhist cultural expansion.

## Japanese Figures

- Varley (2000). Murasaki Shikibu, Sen no Rikyū, Miyamoto Musashi.
- Keene (1976--1999). Literary figures, dates, cultural impact.

## West African Figures

- Levtzion and Pouwels (2000). Mansa Musa, Sundiata Keita.
- Niane, D. T. *Sundiata: An Epic of Old Mali*. Trans. G. D. Pickett. London: Longman, 1965.
  Sundiata as historical/mythological figure, dating.

## Hip-Hop Figures

- Chang (2005). DJ Kool Herc (b. 1955), Afrika Bambaataa, Grandmaster Flash.
- KRS-One. *The Gospel of Hip Hop*. Brooklyn: PowerHouse Books, 2009.
  KRS-One (b. 1965) as cultural philosopher, Temple of Hiphop founding.
