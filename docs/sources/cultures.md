# Culture Sources

References for culture emergence/end dates, geographic roots, status classifications, and endonyms.

## Ancient and Classical

- Shaw, Ian. *The Oxford History of Ancient Egypt*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2000.
  Egyptian culture emergence (c. 3100 BCE), dynastic periodization, end of pharaonic culture (641 CE Arab conquest).

- Brosius, Maria. *The Persians: An Introduction*. London: Routledge, 2006.
  Persian cultural continuity from Achaemenid (550 BCE) through modern Iran. Endonym: Irani/Farsi.

- Boardman, John, et al., eds. *The Cambridge Ancient History*. 14 vols. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1970--2005.
  Greek culture (800 BCE Archaic period), Roman cultural sphere, Hellenistic syncretism.

## South and East Asian

- Basham, A. L. *The Wonder That Was India*. 3rd ed. London: Sidgwick and Jackson, 1967.
  Vedic culture emergence (c. 1500 BCE), sacred texts chronology, practice categories.

- Keay, John. *India: A History*. London: HarperCollins, 2000.
  Indian cultural continuity, regional variation, sacred/secular practice distinction.

- Keene, Donald. *A History of Japanese Literature*. 5 vols. New York: Columbia University Press, 1976--1999.
  Japanese cultural development from Jomon through modern period.

- Varley, Paul. *Japanese Culture*. 4th ed. Honolulu: University of Hawaii Press, 2000.
  Japanese aesthetics, tea ceremony, martial arts as cultural practice.

## African

- Iliffe, John. *Africans: The History of a Continent*. 2nd ed. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2007.
  West African empires, cultural continuity, oral tradition.

- Levtzion, Nehemia, and Randall L. Pouwels, eds. *The History of Islam in Africa*. Athens: Ohio University Press, 2000.
  Mali/Songhai cultural development, Timbuktu as intellectual center.

- Connah, Graham. *African Civilizations*. 3rd ed. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2015.
  Great Zimbabwe, Aksumite Empire, material culture.

## European / Celtic / Norse

- Cunliffe, Barry. *The Ancient Celts*. 2nd ed. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2018.
  Scottish/Celtic culture, emergence dates, Gaelic revival.

- Price, Neil. *The Viking Way*. 2nd ed. Oxford: Oxbow Books, 2019.
  Norse cultural practices, belief systems, material culture.

## Indigenous / Oceanian

- Flood, Josephine. *The Original Australians*. Sydney: Allen and Unwin, 2006.
  Aboriginal Australian cultural continuity (65,000+ years), Dreamtime, oral tradition.

## Modern / Diasporic

- Chang, Jeff. *Can't Stop Won't Stop: A History of the Hip-Hop Generation*. New York: St. Martin's Press, 2005.
  Hip-hop culture emergence (1973, South Bronx), four elements, cultural spread.

- Rose, Tricia. *Black Noise: Rap Music and Black Culture in Contemporary America*. Middletown: Wesleyan University Press, 1994.
  Hip-hop as African American cultural expression, practice categorization.

## Mesoamerican

- Coe, Michael D. *The Maya*. 9th ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 2015.
  Maya cultural continuity, calendar systems, writing, architecture.

- Townsend, Richard F. *The Aztecs*. 3rd ed. London: Thames and Hudson, 2009.
  Aztec/Mexica cultural practices, sacred calendar, material culture.
