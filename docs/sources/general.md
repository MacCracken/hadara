# General References

Standard reference works consulted across all hadara data modules.

## Cultural Anthropology

- Kottak, Conrad Phillip. *Cultural Anthropology: Appreciating Cultural Diversity*. 18th ed. New York: McGraw-Hill, 2019.
  Framework for cultural classification, kinship systems, ritual categories, material culture.

- Ember, Carol R., and Melvin Ember. *Cultural Anthropology*. 14th ed. Upper Saddle River: Pearson, 2015.
  Comparative method, cultural universals, practice categorization.

## World History (civilizational context)

- Stearns, Peter N., ed. *The Encyclopedia of World History*. 6th ed. Boston: Houghton Mifflin, 2001.
  Primary chronological reference for emergence/end dates across all modules.

- Roberts, J. M., and Odd Arne Westad. *The Penguin History of the World*. 6th ed. London: Penguin, 2013.
  Narrative context for cultural trajectories, diffusion, and interaction.

- Diamond, Jared. *Guns, Germs, and Steel*. New York: W. W. Norton, 1997.
  Geographic determinism, cultural exchange, technology diffusion.

## Religion and Sacred Traditions

- Smith, Huston. *The World's Religions*. San Francisco: HarperOne, 1991.
  Sacred practices, texts, and belief systems across major traditions.

- Eliade, Mircea. *The Sacred and the Profane*. New York: Harcourt, 1959.
  Sacred/secular distinction in cultural practice, ritual time vs. profane time.

## Material Culture

- Prown, Jules David. "Mind in Matter: An Introduction to Material Culture Theory and Method." *Winterthur Portfolio* 17, no. 1 (1982): 1--19.
  Framework for artifact classification and cultural significance.

## Music and Performing Arts

- Nettl, Bruno, et al., eds. *The Garland Encyclopedia of World Music*. 10 vols. New York: Routledge, 1998--2002.
  Primary reference for music traditions, instruments, performance practice.

## Linguistics and Naming

- Campbell, Lyle. *Historical Linguistics: An Introduction*. 4th ed. Cambridge: MIT Press, 2021.
  Language classification, endonym/exonym conventions.
